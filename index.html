Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I water my plants?

Watering frequency depends on the type of plant and its specific needs. It's important to check the soil moisture before watering and to adjust based on factors such as season and environmental conditions.

What kind of lighting conditions do these plants require?

Plants have varying light requirements, ranging from full sun to low light. Each plant listing on our website includes information about its preferred lighting conditions to help you find the perfect match for your home.

Do you provide care instructions for your plants?

Yes, we include care instructions with every plant purchase. These instructions cover topics such as watering, sunlight exposure, temperature, and fertilizing, ensuring that you have all the information needed to care for your new plant.

What is your plant return policy?

Our plant return policy allows for returns within 30 days of purchase, provided the plant is in its original condition. Please contact our customer service team to initiate the return process, and we'll be happy to assist you.

How are your plants shipped?

We take great care in packaging and shipping our plants to ensure they arrive safely and in good condition. Plants are securely packaged to prevent damage during transit, and we offer expedited shipping options for faster delivery.
